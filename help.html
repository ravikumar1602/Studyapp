Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on StudyApp?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of the page. Fill in the required information including your name, email address, and create a password. Once submitted, you'll receive a verification email to activate your account.

How can I enroll in a course?

Browse our courses page and click on any course you're interested in. On the course details page, click the "Enroll Now" button. Some courses may be free while others require payment. After enrollment, you can access the course materials immediately.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! StudyApp is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it on your mobile device for easy access. On Android, use the "Add to Home Screen" option in your browser menu. On iOS, use the "Share" button and select "Add to Home Screen".

How do I track my learning progress?

Your learning progress is automatically tracked as you complete lessons and quizzes. You can view your progress by visiting the "My Progress" section in your dashboard. Here you'll see completion percentages, badges earned, and time spent on each course.
